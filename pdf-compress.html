Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PDF compressor free to use?

Yes, our PDF compression tool is completely free to use with no hidden charges. You can compress as many PDF files as you need without any limitations.

How does PDF compression work?

Our tool reduces PDF file size by optimizing images (downsampling and recompressing), removing redundant data, optimizing fonts, and cleaning up document structure without affecting the visible content quality.

Is my data safe when using this tool?

Absolutely. We process your files securely, and all uploaded documents are automatically deleted from our servers after 1 hour. We don't store or inspect your files' content.

What's the maximum file size I can compress?

You can compress PDF files up to 50MB in size. For larger files, we recommend splitting them into smaller parts before compression.

Does compression affect PDF quality?

With our recommended medium compression setting, quality loss is minimal. Higher compression may reduce image quality but keeps text sharp. You can preview the result before downloading.
